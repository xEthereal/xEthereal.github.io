What do we require?
  • 7.5k actions per day on average.
  • 600b gold worth of whatever we need at the time per week.
  • 5% of your income, though we usually leave our tax off to benefit our members.
  • You must be nice to your fellow members, we attempt to maintain a positive attitude in all circumstances.
  • We require that whenever a personal event occurs that will make a your average actions fall, that you inform an officer.
  • We require you to be awesome, inspire awe everywhere you go, as well as in everyone you meet.

If at any point you fall below these requirements an officer will notify you, though we encourage you to handle it independently.

  • We are not aiming to be rank #1 though that would definitely be nice!

  • We want loyal members willing to stick with us during difficult times.
